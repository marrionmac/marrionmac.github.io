About me

I'm an undergraduate student at CSUF studying Psychology. I also work for the Center for Research on Educational Access and Leadership to evaluate and implement institutional programs to better serve underrepresented students, as well as do research with Dr. Jennifer Coons to better understand the relationship between academic and social factors on student success.

Outside of my work, I enjoy trying out new coffee beans, playing with my pug Apollo, and listening to my favorite podcasts! Some of my personal values include diversity, curiosity, and empathy. I learn something new everyday, and I look forward to improving my knowledge of varied areas.
